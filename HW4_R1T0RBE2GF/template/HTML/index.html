CORONAVIRUS

Coronavirus is a virus that is common among animals. It is a dangerous disease with incubation period between 4-6 days . It is fatal especially for those with weakened immune system,the elderly and the young. There are many symptoms. Namely: fever,runny nose,cough,sore throat and possibly a headache. To prevent yourself, you need to wash your hands with soap and water before touching anything including your nose,eyes and mouth ,covering your mouth and nose when coughing and sneezing etc.
